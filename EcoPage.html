RECYCLING MADE EASY




Here in Canada recycling feels like a granted, but in actuality a lot more goes into the recyling process that just tossing your waste in the right bin. Only around 5% of Canadians properly recycle their waste, and that's because a lot of us don't know how to! We know that recycling takes time and effort, so we've complied a database of common recyclable items to make your job easier. We encourage you to do your part, go the extra mile and use RECYCLEAN as a quick tool, with a few questions that will ensure that your efforts don't go to waste!
